Questions & Answers

Get to know everything about Appel, from basic controls to advanced gameplay mechanics

What type of game is Appel?

Appel is a platformer game where players jump, stick to walls, and navigate through levels to collect golden apples and reach the end.

Who created the Appel game?

Appel was created by Griffpatch, a well-known Scratch developer who also made games like Paper Minecraft and Geometry Dash.

What are the basic controls in Appel?

Use WASD or arrow keys to move and jump. Press S or the down arrow key to crouch, and hold the up arrow key for higher jumps.

How does the wall-sticking mechanic work?

When jumping toward a wall, hold the left or right direction key to stick to it. You can then jump off the wall or stick to ceilings by holding the up arrow key.

What collectibles are in the game?

Each level contains golden apples to collect, as well as a hidden giant red apple in every stage.

What are the main mechanics in Appel?

The game includes wall-jumping, crouching to pass through tight spaces, collecting apples, and avoiding spikes and other hazards.

How many levels are there in Appel?

There are 9 levels in total, each featuring unique platform designs, apple placements, and sometimes different color schemes.

How can you jump higher in the game?

Hold the up arrow key for a higher jump. You can also perform wall jumps by pressing up while sticking to a wall.

How do you deal with enemies in Appel?

Jumping on enemies' heads is an effective way to eliminate them while avoiding damage.

What unique level design elements are featured in Appel?

The game includes half-height blocks, rotating tiles, eye-like decorations that follow players, and various traps and obstacles.
